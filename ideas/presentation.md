# Presentation Ideas

## Customer Variety And Representation

### Summary

Broaden the visible customer roster so the crowd feels more varied and more representative of the people who might actually show up at the stand.

### Candidate directions

- Add more customer sprite/body-type/skin-tone variety to the crowd mix.
- Make sure generated or hand-authored customer art avoids a narrow default look.
- Revisit customer casting whenever new art packs or upgraded scene presentation work is added.

### Why this could help

- Makes the simulation feel more human and less repetitive.
- Avoids the crowd presentation feeling unintentionally exclusionary.
- Gives future visual-polish work a clearer quality bar.
